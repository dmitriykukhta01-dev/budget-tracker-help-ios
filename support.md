# How can we help?

Family Budget Tracker is a simple app for tracking your personal and shared family finances. If something isn't working, or you just have a question, we're happy to help.
{: .lead}

> **Contact support:** [up.dreams.dev@gmail.com](mailto:up.dreams.dev@gmail.com)
>
> We typically reply within 2–3 business days. To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include your device model, OS version, and a short description of what happened.

## What you can do with the app

- **Track income &amp; expenses** — record what you earn and spend against a fixed set of categories.
- **Personal &amp; family budgets** — keep a private budget, or share one with up to 5 family members.
- **Analytics** — see where your money goes with clear summaries and charts.
- **Your preferences** — choose your currency, language, and light or dark theme.

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
  <summary>How do I sign in?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>You sign in with your <strong>Google</strong> or <strong>Apple</strong> account — there's no separate password to remember. Tap the sign-in button for your preferred provider and follow the prompts. If you use Sign in with Apple, you can choose to hide your email address.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What's the difference between a personal and a family budget?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>Your <strong>personal budget</strong> is private to you by default. A <strong>family budget</strong> is shared: everyone in the family can see and add entries to it. You can use the app entirely on your own (solo mode) without ever creating a family.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I add or invite family members?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>A family can have up to <strong>5 members</strong>. If you're the family admin, open the family section in the app to invite others. People you invite can join your shared budget; leaving a family always keeps your own personal budget intact.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Does the app connect to my bank?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>No. Family Budget Tracker never connects to your bank, cards, or any financial institution. You enter amounts yourself, which means your real account credentials and balances are never shared with the app.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I change my currency, language, or theme?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>Open <strong>Settings</strong> in the app. There you can set your preferred currency, switch the language, and choose a light, dark, or system-matched theme. Your choices are saved to your account.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I delete my account and data?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>You can delete your account from within the app's Settings. This removes your personal data as described in our <a href="privacy.html">Privacy Policy</a>. If you'd rather we do it for you, email <a href="mailto:up.dreams.dev@gmail.com">up.dreams.dev@gmail.com</a> from the address linked to your account and we'll take care of it.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Is my financial data private and secure?</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>Yes. Traffic is encrypted in transit, and we don't use ads, analytics, or tracking. The only people who can see a shared budget are the family members you choose to share it with. Read the full details in our <a href="privacy.html">Privacy Policy</a>.</p>
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>I found a bug or have a feature request.</summary>
  <div class="body">
    <p>We'd love to hear it. Email <a href="mailto:up.dreams.dev@gmail.com">up.dreams.dev@gmail.com</a> with a description and, for bugs, your device model and OS version so we can reproduce it.</p>
  </div>
</details>
